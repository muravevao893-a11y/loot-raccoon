# Brand: Loot Raccoon

## Association

Hamster Kombat has a hamster. Loot Raccoon has a **loot raccoon**: cute, sneaky, greedy for shiny things, naturally masked, and perfect for meme culture.

Core phrase: **Trash → Treasure**.

## Name ideas

Primary: **Loot Raccoon**  
Russian-friendly: **Лутовый Енот**  
Token draft: **ROON**  
Community: **Roon Crew**

## Visual DNA

- Mood: night alley, rooftops, glowing dumpsters, treasure vaults.
- Palette: black glass, acid cyan, warm gold, magenta highlights.
- Shape language: rounded cards, glowing circles, sticker-like mascot.
- UI vibe: premium Telegram-native game, not cheap casino.

## Mascot personality

The raccoon is not a warrior. He is a clever collector.

- Finds loot in trash and turns it into status.
- Upgrades gadgets: claws, magnets, drones, vaults.
- Forms a crew: friends, guilds, leagues.
- Wants shiny things more than world domination.

## Tone of voice

- Short, punchy, memeable.
- No promises of profit.
- No fake investment language.
- Examples:
  - “Енот вышел на смену.”
  - “Лут сам себя не украдёт.”
  - “Trash → Treasure.”
  - “Банда ждёт апгрейд.”
  - “Очки — это очки. ROON — потом, после проверки.”
